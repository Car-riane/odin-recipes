Creamy Homemade Oatmilk

Creamy oat milk is a dairy-free alternative to traditional milk, made from blending oats with water. It boasts a rich, smooth texture that rivals the creaminess of cow's milk. This plant-based milk is often fortified with vitamins and minerals, making it a healthy and delicious choice for coffee, cereal, or simply enjoyed on its own.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1 cup of dry rolled oats
  • a pinch of salt
  • dash of cinnamon
  • dash of maple syrup
  • a couple of drops of vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Put all the Ingredients into a blender and blend for 30 seconds.
  2. Use a cheescloth to strain it

Enjoy!
